# Complete List of Tax Preparer Permissions

## Total: 16 Permissions

---

## 1. Client Access (2 permissions)
- **`view_assigned_clients_only`** - View assigned clients only
- **`view_all_firm_clients`** - View all firm clients

> **Note:** These two permissions are mutually exclusive - only one can be selected at a time.

---

## 2. Client Management (2 permissions)
- **`create_clients`** - Create new clients
- **`edit_client_data`** - Edit client personal and tax data

---

## 3. Document Management (3 permissions)
- **`upload_documents`** - Upload client documents
- **`download_documents`** - Download client documents
- **`delete_documents`** - Delete client documents

---

## 4. Returns & Filing (1 permission)
- **`view_returns`** - View completed returns and filing status

---

## 5. E-Signatures (2 permissions)
- **`initiate_esignature`** - Initiate e-signature requests
- **`track_esignature`** - Track e-signature requests

---

## 6. Communication (2 permissions)
- **`send_messages`** - Send client communications (messages)
- **`receive_messages`** - Receive client communications (messages)

---

## 7. Billing (1 permission)
- **`create_invoices`** - Create invoices

---

## 8. Reports & Analytics (2 permissions)
- **`access_analytics`** - Access reporting and analytics
- **`export_reports`** - Export client or firm reports

---

## 9. Team Management (1 permission)
- **`manage_team`** - Invite, deactivate, or manage other team members
